Why choose our company for your home repairs?

Welcome to HandyFix, your trusted partner for all your home repair and maintenance needs. We are a team of skilled and experienced professionals dedicated to making your home a better place.

Founded by Emil Stoyanov in 2001, HandyFix has grown from a passion for fixing things arround the house into a thriving business. With more than 20 years of experience in the industry, we have proudly served countless homeowners and businesses in London and surrounding areas, earning a reputation for reliability, quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

Our mission is to provide you with reliable,efficient and affordable solution for all your home improvement projects. Whether it`s a minor repair, a major renovation we approach every job with dedication and commitment to exceeding your expectation.
